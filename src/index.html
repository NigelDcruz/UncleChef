Services

We cook goan food for all ocasions at very affordable prices. Please contact us for prices.

Birthday Party

Birthday's are supposed to be fun. We want you to spend more time with your loved ones than in your kitchen. Leave the kitchen to us without any worry and we'll take care of everything!

Weddings

Weddings are fun! But preparing food takes the fun out of weddings. We understand that and got you covered. Tell us what you need and we'll take care of the rest while you peacefully enjoy your wedding!

Other events

If you like our food and want us to serve you for any event, do get in touch with us. We are happy to serve you and always ready to challange your taste buds. (Although we take last minute orders it's best to inform us 1week before the event)
